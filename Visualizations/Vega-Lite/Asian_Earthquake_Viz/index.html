Seismic Patterns Across Asia: Magnitude, Depth and Impact

Regional Analysis

Earthquakes are mainly concentrated along tectonic plate boundaries, such as the Pacific Ring of Fire and the Himalayan region.

Deeper and stronger earthquakes occur mostly near subduction zones like Japan and Indonesia, while inland areas experience shallower, moderate ones.
